Where was The White Lotus filmed?
Here's a guide to all the hotels featured in Seasons 1, 2, and 3.

By Kyle Marcelino

Golden sands in Hawaii, rolling hills in Italy, and now tropical Thailand — here’s where The White Lotus was filmed (and yes, you can stay there).

White Lotus Season 1 Hawaii Hotel

Four Seasons Resort Maui at Wailea, Hawaii

It all began at the Four Seasons Maui, where The White Lotus introduced its signature blend of luxury and drama against a stunning Hawaiian backdrop.
Beyond its luxe amenities, this five-star resort opens the door to championship golf, pickleball, tennis, and a lively dining and shopping scene. For those
craving adventure, Maui’s stunning landscapes—like the famed Road to Hana—are just a drive away.


White Lotus Season 2 Italy Hotel

San Domenico Palace, Taormina

Season 2 of The White Lotus brings the drama to Sicily, with most of the action set at the breathtaking Four Seasons San Domenico Palace.
With epic views of the Ionian Sea, cliffs that drop into the coast, and Mount Etna looming in the distance, it’s pure cinematic magic.
The dining experience at San Domenico Palace is nothing short of extraordinary, while off the grounds, the adventures are just as cinematic
—think stargazing under Sicilian skies, tasting your way around Mount Etna, cruising the Aeolian Islands, or channeling your inner Monica Vitti!


White Lotus Season 3 Thailand Hotel

Four Seasons Resort Koh Samui, Thailand

Set against the lush backdrop of Koh Samui’s turquoise waters and jungle hills, the Four Seasons resort plays host to the drama and
decadence of The White Lotus's latest season. Perched on a tropical hillside overlooking the Gulf of Thailand, Four Seasons Koh Samui
delivers pure island seclusion. Its 60 villas and 15 private residences offer immersive nature experiences—think flower-scented breezes
and birdsong at dawn. One of the resort’s top features? The Secret Garden Spa is a key highlight, but guests can also engage in immersive
experiences such as ATV tours or visit an elephant sanctuary.
